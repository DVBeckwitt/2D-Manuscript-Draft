\section{Results: ordered films}

We first applied the staged workflow to ordered MBE-grown Bi$_2$Te$_3$ and Bi$_2$Se$_3$ films. The detector images contained the specular features and off-specular arcs expected for a 2D oriented powder with little stacking disorder. These two materials therefore provided the ordered-film benchmark.

  \centering
  \includegraphics[width=\linewidth]{figures/results_ordered/ordered_detector_summary.png}
  \caption{Ordered-film detector-space fits. (a) Bi$_2$Te$_3$ measured, simulated, and residual detector views after the final staged refinement. (b) Corresponding Bi$_2$Se$_3$ detector comparison. (c) Representative local profiles from specular and off-specular detector ROIs used to check the mosaic/profile state after center alignment. (d) Post-fit $Q_z$ checks of ROI-integrated Bragg intensities for the two ordered films. The comparison is made after the detector-space geometry and local line-shape terms have already been fixed.}
  \label{fig:ordered_detector_fits}
\end{figure}

Figure~\ref{fig:ordered_detector_fits} summarizes the final ordered-film fits in the same order as the refinement workflow. The measured/simulated/residual detector panels show that the model placed the specular peaks and off-specular arcs at the observed detector positions for both Bi$_2$Te$_3$ and Bi$_2$Se$_3$. This is the most sensitive check of the beam-center, detector-distance, detector-tilt, and sample-alignment parameters, because a small geometric error would produce peak-position offsets across the image.

The local profile checks then isolated line-shape information from integrated intensity along $\phi$ for the mosaic. The specular-cap and off-specular-arc profiles were reproduced by the same Gaussian-plus-Lorentzian mosaic profile. The final $Q_z$ checks compared the ROI-integrated intensities after the geometry and line-shape parameters had been fixed. Agreement in this view indicated that the ordered structure-factor description was sufficient for these films within the resolution of the present workflow. The important result is that the same refinement remained stable in both systems thus setting the baseline for the PbI$_2$ disorder analysis to come.
